Apple Pie

description

an apple pie is generally double-crusted, with pastry both above and below the filling; the upper crust may be solid or latticed (woven of crosswise strips). The bottom crust may be baked separately ("blind") to prevent it from getting soggy. Deep-dish apple pie often has a top crust only.

ingredients

  • apples
  • lemon juice
  • water
  • brown sugar
  • granulated sugar
  • cornstarch
  • cinnamon
  • nutmeg and butter
